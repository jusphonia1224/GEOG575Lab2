This map is showing the vaccination data retrieved from WHO, which describe the situation of how citizens are equipped with vaccinations.

To have a better comparison for the data, the data which are in terms of 100,000 persons are used in this map.

The map are with 5 categories selected:
1. TOTAL_VACCINATIONS_PER100: This value showing the number of citizens who are vaccinated with at least 1 dose, in terms of 100,000 persons.
2. PERSONS_VACCINATED_1PLUS_DOSE_PER100: This value showing the number of citizens who are vaccinated with more than 1 dose, in terms of 100,000 persons.
3. PERSONS_FULLY_VACCINATED_PER100: This value showing the number of citizens who are fully vaccinated, in terms of 100,000 persons.
4. NUMBER_VACCINES_TYPES_USED: This value showing how many types of vaccinations are allowed to be used in this country.
5. PERSONS_BOOSTER_ADD_DOSE_PER100: This value showing the number of citizens who are vaccinated with the booster dose, in terms of 100,000 persons.
